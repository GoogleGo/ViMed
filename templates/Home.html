ViMED is a medical predictor that takes images and creates a prediction for a diagnosis. It is 100% online, confidential, and automated, with no data being saved at any time.





Why Choose ViMED


Fast Predictions in Seconds

Our algorithms analyze what you give us, and return results in seconds. Each model has been tailored for the task at hand, and will give you accurate predictions in real time.

100% Online, No data saved

All you have to do is upload, and click submit! Everything is handled by our software as we work to get the prediction back to you. Once we're done, the data is deleted off our servers.




Integrations

General Skin Cancer Detection

Do you have a rash, mole, or bump that keeps growing in size? Take a close-up image of it to get a list of possible cancers it may be.

Melanoma Skin Cancer Detection

The most serious type of skin cancer is Melanoma. It is normally characterized by a change in an existing mole, and the development of an unusually pigmented growth. If this applies, please upload a close-up image to get a medical prediction.

COVID Chest Xray Detection

Do you have a chest X-ray? Are you wondering if you had COVID at that time? Upload a picture for a COVID-19 analysis.
